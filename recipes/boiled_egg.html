Boiled Egg

Description

Easy and delicious boiled egg recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggs - as many as you want
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a pan with water to boiling point.
  2. Carrefully put the eggs inside the pan not to break their shells
  3. For a runny yolk, wait 3 to 4 minutes; for a soft one, 4 - 5 minutes; and for hard boiled eggs, wait at least 6 minutes
  4. Throw the hot water out of the pan and pour cold water on the boiled eggs still in the pan to stop the cooking.
  5. Remove the shells and enjoy with some salt.
